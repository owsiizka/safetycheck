About Us

We are a team of aspiring IT specialists who are interested in helping children who may be potential victims of scammers on the Internet. Our site will help you find out which phrases in online correspondence may raise suspicions

Why was this website created?

Netizens are often exposed to danger on the Internet. There are many criminals on social networks who sit behind anonymous accounts in order to obtain intimate photos and the like through blackmail. Solving this problem is very important, because most often children aged 9-12 years old face this problem. And therefore this site was created with the aim of maintaining the safety of children on the Internet and solving this problem.
